    Opening Times and Prices

    Full Day Doggy Daycare - Monday to Friday, 7:30am to 6:00pm - GBP 30

    Half Day Doggy Daycare - Monday to Friday, 7:30am to 12:30pm or 1:00pm to 6:00pm - GBP 20

    Important Information

    Your pooch is required to attend a free 2 hour assessment prior to your first booking so we can assess their needs and confirm daycare suitability.

    Please download and return registration, consent, and acceptance forms before booking.

    For safety and prevention of infectious diseases, we require evidence that inoculations (including boosters) are up to date and worming has been completed within three months.
