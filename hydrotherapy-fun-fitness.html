    Let Your Dog Splash and Play

    Is your dog a water lover? Then our fun and fitness sessions are perfect for them.

    Our heated hydrotherapy pool provides a safe and fun environment for dogs to swim, play, and socialise.

    Note: Owners are required to be present for all hydrotherapy sessions.

    Contact us

    Benefits of Fun & Fitness Hydrotherapy

    • Exercise and fitness: Low-impact workouts for cardiovascular health and muscle building.
    • Mental stimulation: Water play helps prevent boredom.
    • Socialisation: Controlled interaction with other dogs.
    • Confidence building: Helps dogs become confident in water.
    • Stress relief: Swimming can release energy and reduce stress.

    Our Fun & Fitness Sessions

    Sessions are supervised by our certified hydrotherapist to ensure safety and wellbeing.

    We welcome dogs of all ages, breeds, and swimming abilities.
