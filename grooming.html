    Our Dog Grooming Services

    We provide complete canine grooming services from maintenance visits to full restyle appointments.

    • De-Shedding: Specialised treatment designed to reduce shedding and improve coat condition.
    • Bathing & Drying: A luxurious bathing and drying service for a fresh, pampered finish.
    • Styling: Breed-specific cuts, clipping, and scissoring for the right look.
    • Nail Clipping: Professional nail care for healthy paws.
    • Hand Stripping: Expert hand stripping for wire-haired coats.
    • Eye & Ear Cleaning: Hygiene care to keep your dog comfortable.
    • Anal Gland Expression: Professional care to prevent discomfort from impacted glands.
