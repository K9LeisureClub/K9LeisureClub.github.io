About K9 Leisure Club

Born from a passion for paws

K9 Leisure Club combines structured daycare, grooming, and hydrotherapy services for dogs in Kidderminster and nearby areas.

Our Stor

1

Our passion for paws runs deep. Laura, with years of experience as a dog groomer and kennel manager, understands the unique needs and quirks of every furry friend.

2

Combined with Andy's business acumen and shared love for animals, we've created a dream team dedicated to providing exceptional care and creating a thriving environment for dogs to flourish.

3

We're committed to giving back to the community — proudly offering free hydrotherapy slots to local rescue centres and providing placements for students eager to learn about canine care.

A Place to Play, Relax, and Heal

K9 Leisure Club is more than just a doggy daycare – it's a place where tails wag freely and adventures abound!

Daycare Delights

Our unique daycare setup offers the best of both worlds: individual kennels for downtime and a spacious play area for socialising and exercise. We ensure every dog gets the perfect balance of rest and play throughout the day.

Grooming with a View

Our tranquil grooming room offers a pampering experience like no other. While your dog enjoys a luxurious groom, you can relax and watch the world go by, knowing your furry friend is in expert hands.

Hydrotherapy Haven

Our state-of-the-art hydrotherapy centre is the heart of K9 Leisure Club. Whether your dog needs rehabilitation, fitness training, or simply a fun swim, our customised sessions provide a wealth of benefits in a safe and supportive environment.
