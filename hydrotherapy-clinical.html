    Helping Your Dog Heal and Thrive

    At K9 Leisure Club, we understand that sometimes our furry friends need a little extra help to recover from injury or manage chronic conditions.

    Our clinical hydrotherapy services are designed to provide targeted rehabilitation and support for dogs facing a variety of health challenges.

    Note: Owners are required to be present for all hydrotherapy sessions.

    Contact us

    Conditions We Can Help With

    • Post-surgical rehabilitation after orthopaedic procedures.
    • Arthritis management to reduce pain and improve mobility.
    • Neurological conditions support for balance and strength.
    • Obesity management for safe weight loss and fitness.
    • Muscle injuries and strains support to promote healing.

    Our Clinical Hydrotherapy Approach

    Our certified hydrotherapist will work with you and your veterinarian to develop a customised treatment plan for your dog.

    • Underwater treadmill therapy to improve gait, strength, and endurance.
    • Therapeutic exercises to target muscle groups and improve range of motion.
    • Manual therapy including massage and stretching to relieve tension.
